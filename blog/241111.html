Getting into a new hobby: Taking pictures

Nov 11, 2024

I recently got into a new hobby, which is photography. At first, I wasn't sure I would like it. Everyone seems to like taking so many photos nowadays, while I'm not too keen on that. I did view some guides on the Internet. It taught me a few things about aperture, ISO, shutter speed, though that's still quite hard to memorize. There were Rule of Thirds, Leading Lines and Curves, Symmetry and Balance, etc.

Last Saturday, I went to District 2 Connecting Bridge and took some pictures for experimentation. The results were staggering. They surprised me. They came out good. Maybe a more professional photographer might criticize me but hey, I quite like this new venture. So now, I'm expanding my blog to include this category, and many more to come if I'm interested.
